# Privacy Policy for Relay — AI Context Bridge

**Effective Date:** June 9, 2026

Relay ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and stored when you use the Relay Chrome Extension.

## 1. Information We Collect
Relay is designed to help you transfer conversation context between different AI platforms. To achieve this, the extension locally accesses the text of your conversations on supported AI websites (such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) when you explicitly interact with the extension.

## 2. How Your Information is Used
The collected conversation context is used **exclusively** to facilitate the core functionality of the extension: injecting your previous conversation history into a new AI platform chat window so you can continue your workflow seamlessly.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
**100% Local Storage:** All conversation data captured by Relay is stored locally on your device within your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). 
**No External Transmission:** Your data is **never** transmitted to our servers, external databases, or any third-party services. The extension does not make any external network requests. 

## 4. Information Sharing
We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information or conversation data with any third parties. Since the data never leaves your device, it is physically impossible for us to share it.

## 5. Chrome Web Store Limited Use Policy
Relay's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs and browser APIs adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:
- We only use your data to provide or improve your user-facing features.
- We do not use or transfer your data for serving ads, including retargeting, personalized, or interest-based advertising.
- We do not allow humans to read your data (as it is solely stored on your local device).

## 6. Your Rights and Control
You have full control over your data. You can clear your stored conversation history at any time directly through the Relay extension's interface (via the "Clear Context" button in the popup or floating panel). Uninstalling the extension will also automatically delete all associated local storage data.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted in this repository.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please open an issue in this GitHub repository.
